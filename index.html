100 light switches

There are 100 light switches with two states, on and off. They all begin being turned off. One by one, 100 people flip some amount of switches and changes its state to the opposite of what it was after the previous person flipped their chosen switches. The 1st person flips every switch. The 2nd person flips every 2nd switch. The 3rd person flips every 3rd switch, and so on until the 100th person flips every 100th switch.

Which of the light switches are turned on after every person has flipped their chosen switches?
